MIDNIGHT

Stillness — 静寂

In stillness, it waits.

A one-of-a-kind katana sleeps on volcanic rock. Keep scrolling: the steel is about to wake.

Ascension — 上昇

The blade rises on its own.

Watch the stand: it crumbles to sand beneath her. Nothing holds her now.

The edge — 刃文

Come close to the edge.

1600 layers of folded steel. The waving line is the hamon: the scar of the fire that tempered her.

Ignition — 青き炎

The steel remembers the forge.

Blue fire runs the blade from guard to tip. It burns without consuming.

Spirit — 魂

Blade and sheath, face to face.

The saya returns from the void and they spin together, bound by something older than steel.

The fall — 一撃

A single cut.

The stone returns to receive her. The whole weight of the sky on the tip.

Legend — 伝説

Planted, she remains.

Burning blue upon the rock. Waiting for a hand worthy of her.
